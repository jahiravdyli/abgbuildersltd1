Our Story

Built From
the Ground Up

ABG Builders was founded with a clear mission: to provide Londoners with a construction company they could genuinely trust. One that shows up on time, delivers what it promises, and takes real pride in the quality of every finished project.

Over the years we've grown into one of London's most respected building firms, completing hundreds of projects across the capital — from small decorating jobs to complex multi-storey new builds. But our values have never changed.

We work with homeowners, landlords, property developers and architects, always bringing the same commitment to quality, communication and craftsmanship — whatever the size of the project.

What We Stand For

Our Values

01
Quality First

We never cut corners. Every project is built to the highest standard using quality materials and skilled tradespeople, with the finish inspected before we consider the job done.

02
Honest Communication

From your first call to the final handover, we keep you informed at every stage. Clear quotes, straight answers, and no surprises — that's how we do business.

03
Respect for Your Home

We treat every property we work in with the care and respect it deserves. Tidy sites, protection for your belongings, and considerate working hours are non-negotiables for us.

04
Delivering on Time

We set realistic timelines and work hard to meet them. Delays cost our clients time and money, and we take that seriously.
