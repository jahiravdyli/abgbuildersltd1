Our Services

Every project is handled end-to-end by our experienced team — from planning and design through to the final finishing touches.

🏗️
Loft Conversions

Transform unused roof space into beautiful, functional living areas. Dormer, hip-to-gable, mansard — we handle every type with full planning support.

🏠
Extensions

Single and double-storey extensions that seamlessly blend with your existing home, maximising space and adding significant value to your property.

🔨
Refurbishments

Full and partial property refurbishments — kitchens, bathrooms, whole-house renovations. We breathe new life into existing spaces.
