2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you give us

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you submit a quote request or contact form
  • Property address and details about your proposed project (e.g. type of work, photos, planning documents) when you request a quote
  • Any other information you choose to share with us by phone, email, or in person, including during site visits and project discussions
  • Billing and payment details if you become a client (we do not store full card details — these are processed securely by our payment provider)

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited, collected via cookies and similar technologies (see Section 6)
  • Usage data such as how you interact with our website, for example through analytics tools

2.3 Information from third parties

  • Information from review platforms or social media if you contact us through those channels
  • Information from architects, subcontractors, or local authorities where relevant to a project you've engaged us for

4. Who we share your information with

  • Subcontractors and trades we engage to deliver your project, limited to what's needed to carry out the work
  • Service providers such as our website host, email provider, accounting software, and payment processor
  • Insurers for the purposes of public liability and project insurance
  • Local authorities or building control where required for planning permission or building regulations compliance
  • Professional advisers such as accountants or solicitors where necessary
  • Regulators or law enforcement where we are legally required to do so

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Where your data is stored

We primarily store and process data within the UK. Where a supplier (such as a cloud hosting or email provider) processes data outside the UK or EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as the UK's International Data Transfer Agreement or adequacy regulations.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Make the site function correctly
  • Understand how visitors use the site (analytics, e.g. Google Analytics)

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect how parts of the site work.

If you use analytics, advertising pixels, or chat widgets on the site, you should add a cookie consent banner and list the specific cookies in use here, including their names, purposes, and durations, to fully comply with UK PECR/cookie rules.

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, in certain circumstances
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Request transfer of your data (data portability)
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where we rely on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@abgbuildersltd.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you're unhappy with how we've handled your data.

9. Keeping your data secure

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse, including secure storage, access controls, and working only with reputable third-party providers.

10. Children's privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
