Loft Conversions

Loft
Conversions

Transform your unused roof space into a beautiful, functional living area. A loft conversion is one of the most cost-effective ways to add living space and significant value to your home — without touching your garden.

ABG Builders has extensive experience with all types of loft conversion across London's diverse housing stock, from Victorian terraces to 1960s semis.

  • Dormer, hip-to-gable & mansard conversions
  • Velux / rooflight conversions
  • Full planning application support
  • Building regulations sign-off included
  • Staircases, en-suites & full fit-out
